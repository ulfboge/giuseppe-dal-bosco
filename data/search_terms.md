## Platform-by-Platform Guide

---

### LinkedIn Jobs

Best for: NGOs, consulting firms, private sector, some multilaterals.

**Recommended searches:**
- `"forest restoration" specialist`
- `"forest landscape restoration" advisor`
- `"sustainable forest management" senior`
- `"plantation forestry" manager`
- `"natural forest" management specialist`
- `"silviculture" advisor OR consultant`
- `"nature-based solutions" forestry`
- `"reforestation" programme manager`
- `"FSC" forestry specialist`
- `"forest carbon" technical`
- `"REDD+" forestry specialist`
- `"timber" investment forestry`
- `"NbS" forestry OR "NBS" forestry`

**Filters to use:** All locations → International, Remote; Seniority: Mid-Senior, Director; Job type: Full-time, Contract

### Indeed (International)

Best for: Commercial forestry, consulting, some NGO roles.

**Recommended searches:**
- `tropical forestry specialist`
- `plantation forestry manager`
- `forest restoration advisor`
- `silviculture manager tropical`
- `sustainable forestry consultant`
- `forest operations manager international`

**Location tip:** Search with "worldwide" or leave blank; filter by "Remote" or specific regions (Latin America, Africa, Asia-Pacific).

## Boolean Search String (for LinkedIn / advanced search)

```
("forest restoration" OR "forest landscape restoration" OR "reforestation" OR "plantation forestry" OR "natural forest management" OR "sustainable forest management" OR "silviculture") AND ("specialist" OR "advisor" OR "manager" OR "consultant" OR "officer") AND ("tropical" OR "international" OR "senior")
```
