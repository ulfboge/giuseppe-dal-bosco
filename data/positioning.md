> "Senior forestry professional with 27+ years delivering plantation management, natural forest operations, and technical due diligence for investors across Latin America, Asia, Africa, and the South Pacific."

**Best suited for:**
- Private equity / timberland investment firms (e.g., IFC, GEF, Mirova, New Forests)

- Consulting firms serving forest investors
