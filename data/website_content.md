## Professional Summary

_~120 words. Suitable for webpage "About" section and LinkedIn summary._

Giuseppe Dal Bosco is a Forest Engineer with over 27 years of experience in tropical and sub-tropical forestry across more than 10 countries. His work spans the full forestry cycle — from nursery and silviculture through to harvesting, certification, and strategic asset management.

He has managed large-scale state-owned plantation operations as Chief Executive Officer, delivered monitoring and evaluation programmes for international restoration initiatives, and provided technical and financial advisory to forest investors and development organisations.

Currently based in Hasbergen, Lower Saxony (Germany), Giuseppe is open to international roles — either employed or on contract — in sustainable forest management, forest landscape restoration, nature-based solutions, or forest investment and asset management.

He works fluently in English, Spanish, and Italian.

## Selected Experience Themes

_Short narrative blocks for each thematic area. Use in a timeline or cards layout._

### Large-Scale Plantation Management
As CEO of Fiji Hardwood Corporation Limited, managed sustainable forest operations across 60,000 hectares of state-owned mahogany plantations in the Fiji Islands — including reforestation, silviculture, and harvesting operations.

### Forest Landscape Restoration
At KAYA, led the planning and modelling of forestry and landscape restoration programmes, with responsibility for assessment and monitoring of plantation forestry and natural forest management operations, and coordination of field operations with local partners.

### Monitoring & Evaluation — International Programmes
At Rainforest Alliance (TREES Programme), evaluated and monitored 13 forestry projects across 4 countries in Central America and the Caribbean, with donor reporting to USAID, IDB, and European foundations.

### Technical & Financial Modelling
Developed technical and financial models for plantation and natural forest investments — including carbon forestry — for clients in Latin America, Europe, and globally through Dal Bosco Technical Services.

### Forest Certification & Standards Compliance
Deep experience with FSC Forest Management and Chain of Custody certification (standards SW-FM/COC-1450 and SW-COC-939), including compliance management and assessment processes across multiple geographies.

### Investor Due Diligence
Delivers comprehensive forest asset valuations, wood-flow projections, and technical feasibility assessments for investors and development finance institutions operating in plantation and natural forest contexts.
