About

Professional Summary

Giuseppe Dal Bosco is a Forest Engineer with over 27 years of experience in tropical and sub-tropical forestry across more than 10 countries. His work spans the full forestry cycle — from nursery and silviculture through to harvesting, certification, and strategic asset management.


He has managed large-scale state-owned plantation operations as Chief Executive Officer, delivered monitoring and evaluation programmes for international restoration initiatives, and provided technical and financial advisory to forest investors and development organisations.


Currently based in Hasbergen, Lower Saxony (Germany), Giuseppe is open to international roles — either employed or on contract — in sustainable forest management, forest landscape restoration, nature-based solutions, or forest investment and asset management.
